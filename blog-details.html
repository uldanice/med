List of Countries without Coronavirus case

As the COVID-19 pandemic continues to impact nations worldwide, there are a few countries that have successfully managed to remain free of reported coronavirus cases. These countries have implemented stringent measures such as border closures, strict quarantine protocols, and robust testing strategies to prevent the spread of the virus within their borders. While the situation is subject to change and ongoing vigilance is necessary, as of [current date], the following countries have reported zero cases of COVID-19:

Kiribati Marshall Islands Micronesia Nauru North Korea Palau Samoa Solomon Islands Tonga Turkmenistan Tuvalu Vanuatu It's important to note that limited testing capacity, geographical isolation, and other factors may contribute to underreporting in some regions. Additionally, ongoing surveillance and adherence to public health guidelines are essential to maintaining the status of these countries as coronavirus-free.
